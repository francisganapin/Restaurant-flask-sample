Green Beans with Bacon and New Potatoes

Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices bacon
  • 1 medium onion, chopped (scrubbed clean with a vegetable brush under running water)
  • 2 lbs fresh green beans, snapped (gently rubbed under cold running water)
  • 8 small new potatoes, halved (scrubbed clean with a vegetable brush under running water)
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp pepper
